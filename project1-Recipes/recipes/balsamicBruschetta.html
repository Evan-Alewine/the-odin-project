Balsamic Bruschetta

Description

Bruschetta is a classic Italian appetizer that is easy to make at home. Toasted bread is topped with a mixture of juicy tomatoes, garlic, Parmesan cheese, fresh basil, and balsamic vinegar.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of French Bread cut into 1/4th in. slices
  • 1 2/3rds tbs. Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
  • 8 Diced Roma Tomatoes
  • 1/3rd cup of Chopped Basil
  • 1 oz. of Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 cloves of Minced Garlic
  • 1 tbs. Balsamic Vinegar
  • 1/4th tsp. Kosher Salt
  • 1/4th tsp. Ground Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Brush bread slices lightly with 1 tbs of olive oil, and place onto a baking sheet. Toast the bread until golden, turning halfway through.
  3. While the bread toasts, toss together the tomatoes, basil, parmesan, and garlic in a bowl.
  4. Mix in the balsamic vinegar, the remaining olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spoon mixture onto the toasted bread slices
